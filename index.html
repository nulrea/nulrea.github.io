The formula of the XP capacity, where your LEVEL is x:

f(x) = 20×(floor(2x×1.052x-1)+floor((2x+1)×1.052x))

g(x, a) = 10a×round(x/10a)

XP needed = g(f(x), floor(log10f(x))-1)
